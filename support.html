Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan documents?
Open the app and tap the scan button. You can take 1-20 photos of your documents. The AI will automatically process them and create study materials.
What's included in the free version?
Free users get 3 document scans per month, access to basic study tools, flashcards, summaries, and 2 quiz attempts per document. All features work offline once content is generated.
How do I upgrade to Premium?
Go to Settings â†’ Subscription in the app. Premium gives you unlimited scans, unlimited quiz attempts, and priority AI processing.
Can I use the app offline?
Yes! Once your documents are processed, you can study offline. Flashcards, summaries, and quizzes all work without internet. New scans require internet for AI processing.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings â†’ Account â†’ Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data within 30 days. You can also contact support for immediate deletion.
Is my data secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard encryption for all data. Your documents are processed securely through Firebase and OpenAI. We never share your personal data with third parties.

Common Issues

Scanning Problems

  • Poor OCR results: Ensure good lighting and clear, focused photos
  • Processing fails: Check your internet connection and try again
  • App crashes during scan: Try reducing the number of photos per batch

Subscription Issues

  • Premium not activated: Restore purchases in Settings â†’ Subscription
  • Billing questions: Managed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store
  • Refund requests: Contact Apple or Google support directly

Study Features

  • Flashcards not saving progress: Ensure you're logged in and have internet for sync
  • Quiz won't start: Free users have 2 attempts per document
  • Summaries incomplete: Longer documents may take time to process fully
