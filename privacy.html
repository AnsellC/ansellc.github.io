Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 31, 2025

Introduction

Learnix AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, password, and profile information
  • Study Content: Documents, photos, and materials you upload through the app
  • User-Generated Content: Notes, annotations, and study progress data
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Apple App Store or Google Play Store

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, and study patterns
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Analytics Data: App performance, crash reports, and usage statistics via Firebase Analytics
  • Location Data: General location for analytics purposes (if permitted)

Information from Third Parties

  • Authentication Services: If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive basic profile information
  • Payment Processors: Transaction data from Apple/Google for subscription management

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our AI-powered study tools
  • Process your uploaded documents and generate study materials using OpenAI's services
  • Sync your data across devices through Firebase
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions via RevenueCat
  • Send important service notifications and updates
  • Improve our app through analytics and user feedback
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in limited circumstances:

Service Providers

  • Firebase (Google): Cloud storage, authentication, and analytics
  • OpenAI: AI processing of your study materials (anonymized)
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and payment processing
  • Apple/Google: Payment processing and app distribution

Legal Requirements

  • To comply with legal obligations, court orders, or government requests
  • To protect our rights, property, and safety, and that of our users
  • In connection with a business transfer or merger

With Your Consent

  • When you explicitly consent to sharing information with third parties
  • When you choose to share study materials with other users

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication through Firebase
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls limiting who can access personal data
  • Secure cloud infrastructure through Google Cloud Platform

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Account information until you delete your account
  • Study materials and progress data as long as your account is active
  • Analytics data in aggregated form for service improvement
  • Legal compliance data as required by law

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

  • View and manage your account information within the app
  • Download your study materials and data
  • Delete specific documents or study materials
  • Manage notification preferences

Account Deletion

  • Request complete account deletion through app settings
  • All personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Some anonymized analytics data may be retained for service improvement

Data Portability

  • Export your study materials in common formats
  • Request a copy of your personal data we have collected

Children's Privacy

Learnix AI is intended for users 4 years of age and older. For users under 13, parental consent and supervision is required. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal information without parental consent, we will promptly delete such information.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the app
  • Sending an email notification (if you have provided an email address)
  • Displaying a prominent notice in the app
